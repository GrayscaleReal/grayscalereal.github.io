Music

- FNF-Based Music (Please note that some recent songs aren't on here because I don't have permission to share them just yet.)

- That game with friends


Candy Corn

Made as a Halloween song for "Broken Memories". It's fairly recent, being in July of 2025. A bit messy in some parts but it applies a lot of stuff I learned from studying music and stuff I've tested with songs for other mods.


Fruitcake

Made as a Christmas song for "Broken Memories" (Christmas of '24). The basic premise was Gray and Specter alone in Specter's penthouse, but with Suvo and Carb coming in later in the song.


Golden Carrot

Another song for "Broken Memories". This one was a combination of wanting a song between the primary characters from "FNF: Colorless" and the desire to make another song using Minecraft noises.


Reprinted

One of my personal favorite songs from "FNF: Colorless". It's a remake of a remake of a complete overhaul of a song made for another mod.
For more content from "FNF: Colorless", please check out this video.
It saves me time trying to add everything to this page.


BLACK TIDE (SHIFTED MIX)

Inkblot's "Last Life Chase Theme". It takes a few motifs from LURE and makes pretty heavy use of Inkblot's motif, and uses a sample or two from the former.


Moonstone Point

An ambient theme for Moonstone Point. I don't have much else to say about it other than I really like how it turned out. I feel like it's a good blend of foreboding and comforting.


TAPESTOP

Specter's "Last Creature Standing" theme. Out of everything in this section at the time of writing this description, this is the most recent song. It has references to Double Agent, Sleepless, and BLACK TIDE (SHIFTED MIX).


Black Tide

The original version of Black Tide, made quite a while before the Shifted Mix. This was originally going to be used for a Minecraft mod, and would play while Inkblot was hunting you down. Inkblot themselves originated from this mod as well, but was integrated into the rest of their titular universe.


Double Agent

Specter's theme. Most of this one doesn't hold up as well as some of the others, as it's a bit too repetitive and disjointed, but the reversed part is still pretty good.


Eepless

Some kind of elevator music version of Sleepless. I don't remember why I made this.


LURE

This one's pretty old, having been made in June of 2023. Having originally been made for Beast (one of my EXEs), it was later repurposed for Inkblot when I started stepping back a bit from the EXE community. It could use a few touch-ups here and there but I think it holds up fairly well.


Sleepless

Gray's theme. I'm not quite sure what I was doing this this one, probably because it was made at 4AM after having not slept the night prior, but I like how it turned out.

The name itself is a double meaning, referencing both the fact that Gray can't sleep and the fact that I didn't sleep.
